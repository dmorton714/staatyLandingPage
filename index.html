FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is STAATY?

STAATY is a professional sports analytics platform built for everyday fans. We turn complex sports data into clear, understandable insights through visualizations, probability models, and simple explanations. Built for the most casual of viewers, team fanatics, data nerds and those curious or serious about betting markets. STAATY helps YOU experience the game more clearly.

Who is STAATY Built For?

STAATY is designed for the full spectrum of sports fans:

  • Casual viewers who want quick, clear insights
  • Fantasy sports players tracking performance trends
  • Data-minded fanatics who enjoy deeper analytics
  • Sports bettors looking for transparent probability models

How is STAATY different from existing sites?

Most sports platforms fall into three categories: oversimplified media talking heads, betting sites with no insights, or overwhelming spreadsheets. STAATY is THE BRIDGE to this gap. We combine clean visualizations, simple explanations, and deeper analytics so users can quickly understand what the data actually says.

Is STAATY a sportsbook?

No. STAATY does not take bets or operate as a sportsbook…YET. Our platform focuses on analysis, probability, and transparency. We help everyday fans understand the data behind games and betting markets.

Does STAATY guarantee winning bets?

No platform can guarantee winning bets. What STAATY does provide is analytics-driven probability insights so users can better understand how likely an outcome actually is and whether current odds reflect the underlying data. STAATY provides quick stats to educate the wagerer on their decision vs. gut picks.

Why focus on probability and transparency?

Sports betting has grown rapidly, but most platforms prioritize engagement and wager odds rather than understanding. STAATY is built around a different philosophy: informed fans make better decisions. We help fans approach sports data with confidence instead of speculation.

What sports will STAATY cover?

STAATY will focus on major professional sports leagues, E-sports competitions, and emerging leagues, including women’s professional sports. Part of our mission is building robust, consistent data systems for leagues that currently lack strong public analytics.

Is STAATY free to use?

Yes. Our goal is to make core insights accessible without paywalls. As the platform grows, a multi-tiered subscription service offering advanced tools will be introduced. Eventually, STAATY will offer the ultimate Bloomberg Sports Data Terminal.

Who built STAATY?

STAATY was created by a team of data scientists and analysts who have spent their careers building systems where accuracy and accountability mattered. They partnered with a visionary developer to bring that same approach to sports analytics.
